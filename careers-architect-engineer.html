Architect Engineer

Senior Project Manager – Data Center Development

Position Overview

We are a leading data center development company seeking a Senior Project Manager with direct experience in designing and delivering large‑scale infrastructure projects. This role requires a strong background in CSA (Civil, Structural, and Architectural) design, construction execution, and regulatory permitting, along with the ability to collaborate effectively with MEP disciplines and global stakeholders in English.

Key Responsibilities

  • Manage data center projects from due diligence through design development, permitting, construction and turnover.
  • Manage overall project design, schedules, budgets, and risks.
  • Oversee overall permitting processes, collaborating closely with local authorities and regulatory bodies.
  • Manage technical reviews and documentation to ensure permitting compliance.
  • Optimize designs for constructability, sustainability, and structural resilience in coordination with MEP disciplines.
  • Provide oversight of vendor selection, tender reviews, and contractor performance.
  • Manage teams of engineering and technical staff, mentoring individuals and driving high performance to achieve organizational objectives.
  • Establish and develop design standard and budget management tool for data center development.
  • Conduct technical meetings and communicate effectively with international clients and partners in English.

Required Experience and Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Architecture or Engineering
  • A minimum of 10 years of experience on large-scale infrastructure, commercial, industrial, complex technical, or mission-critical projects such as data centers
  • Proven experience managing CSA permitting, including regulatory engagement and submissions, in coordination with MEP engineering disciplines
  • Solid project management experience and skills for handling multiple projects and stakeholder interfaces
  • Proficiency with CSA engineering and project management tools (e.g., AutoCAD, Revit, Primavera, Procore) as well as the MS Office Suite
  • Ability to find creative and innovative solutions to reduce costs while managing impact to quality, cost, and schedule.
  • Proficiency in English communication for technical discussions, documentation, and client interaction
  • Candidates must have no restrictions on international business travel

Location

  • Seoul headquarters or metropolitan project sites
  • Occasional overseas travel may be required
