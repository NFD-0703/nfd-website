Mechanical & Electrical Engineer

Senior Mechanical & Electrical Engineer – Data Center Development

Position Overview

We are a leading data center development company seeking a Senior Mechanical and Electrical Engineer with direct experience in designing and delivering large-scale infrastructure projects. This role requires a strong background in mechanical and electrical system design, construction execution, and commissioning (Cx), along with the ability to collaborate across disciplines and communicate effectively in English with global stakeholders.

  • Direct expertise in land acquisition and deal execution, including engagement, negotiation, and closing of site transactions.
  • A deep understanding of feasibility studies, development processes, and the data center industry.
  • Strong ability to collaborate across disciplines (engineering, legal, finance) to deliver complex projects.
  • Excellent communication skills in both Korean and English, enabling effective interaction with global stakeholders.

Key Responsibilities

  • Originate and evaluate investment opportunities in data center projects
  • Land deal sourcing, structuring, due diligence, and negotiation of key terms and transaction documents
  • Participate in development processes including land acquisition, permitting, and setting a construction schedule from an investor’s perspective
  • Collaborate across technical, commercial, and legal teams to ensure project alignment and investment protection
  • Prepare investment memorandum, IC presentation and on-going reports for stakeholders and debtors

Required Experience and Qualifications

  • 10-15 years of experience in real estate development, with direct involvement in land acquisition and deal execution
  • Proven track record in sourcing, structuring, and closing land or infrastructure transactions, preferably including data center or industrial projects.
  • Solid understanding of feasibility analysis, permitting, zoning, and development processes from both commercial and technical perspectives
  • Familiarity with project financing, capital structuring, and risk management for real-estate assets
  • Fluent written and verbal communication skills in both Korean and English
  • Candidates must have no restrictions on international business travel

Location

  • Seoul headquarters or metropolitan project sites
  • Occasional overseas travel may be required
